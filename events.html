MAIN EVENTS

SLS: Street league skateboarding

Where precision meets pressure.Defining the limits of technical street skating.

The SLS series is the premier circuit that defined modern competitive street skating. Created to elevate the sport's professionalism, it standardized scoring and introduced a high-stakes, instant-feedback format. SLS fundamentally changed how street contests are judged, moving away from subjective formats to focus on objective consistency and difficulty.

Riders compete on custom, state-of-the-art courses designed to maximize technical difficulty and allow for diverse trick selection on rails, stairs, and ledges.

X Games

The festival of gravity-defying maneuvers.High stakes, high risk, and global spectacle.

X Games is the world's most visible and influential extreme sports festival. Launched in 1995, it transformed action sports into a major media spectacle, prioritizing risk, innovation, and sheer entertainment value. Its coverage is global, bringing skate culture, particularly its most daring aspects, to the widest possible audience year after year.

The event showcases the full spectrum of competitive skateboarding, including high-stakes Street and Park competitions, but is most famous for its high-flying Vert and Big Air disciplines.

THE OLYMPIC GAMES

The ultimate validation of skate's athletic merit.Bringing street and park disciplines to the world's biggest stage.

Skateboarding's inclusion in the Olympic Games marked the ultimate step in its mainstream acceptance and global legitimacy as a sport. Debuting in Tokyo 2020, the event provided the largest, most prestigious platform possible for the world's top skaters.

The Olympic format is strict, rewarding overall performance and consistency across runs. The presence of national teams and standardized judging validated skateboarding's athletic merit, while retaining the sport's cultural vibrancy.

HALL OF FAME:

The Architects of the Culture

The history of skateboarding is written in concrete, plywood, and gravity-defying moments. This section is dedicated to the legends who didn't just master the sport, but fundamentally shaped it.

From the pioneers who took to the empty pools of 1970s California to the technical innovators who unlocked the potential of flat ground, these are the athletes whose names are synonymous with progression, style, and defiance.

They laid the groundwork for every trick, every spot, and every style we celebrate today.

We honor the individuals who turned a simple wooden board into a global cultural phenomenon, elevating a hobby into an art form and a competitive sport.

Dive deep into the archives to revisit the milestone moments that changed trick standards forever, the groundbreaking contest runs that clinched titles, and the sheer persistence and vision that defined their legendary careers.

The Legends of the Deck serve as both powerful inspiration for the next generation. Their legacy endures in the lasting impact they had on global street culture, board design, and the collective spirit of the worldwide skateboarding community.
